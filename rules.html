Rules & Regulations

Please read the following rules and regulations carefully. These guidelines are designed to ensure a safe, respectful, and enjoyable living environment for all residents.

  • Students must inform warden and collect the gate pass incase of moving out for more than 3-4 hours .
  • Students are expected to behave courteously and fairly with everyone, including fellow residents, staff, and visitors
  • Silence must be maintained in the hostel premises, especially after 11:00 PM.
  • Visitors are allowed only during designated hours and must register at the reception.
  • Alcohol, drugs, and smoking are strictly prohibited.
  • Residents are responsible for keeping their rooms and common areas clean.
  • Any damage to hostel property will be charged to the responsible resident(s).
  • Residents must adhere to the hostel timings and curfew hours.
  • Mess timings will be -: 1). Breakfast from 7:00am to 9:00am 2). Lunch from 1:00pm to 3:00pm 3). Evening Snacks from 5:00pm to 6:00pm 4). Dinner from 7:00pm to 9:00pm

  • Entry to the hostel is permitted only till 9:00 PM. Late entry requires prior permission from the warden.
  • Male visitors are not allowed inside the hostel rooms.
  • Residents must inform the warden in case of night-outs or leaves.
  • Use of electrical appliances (heaters, irons, etc.) is allowed only in designated areas.
  • Residents must sign the attendance register daily.

  • Entry to the hostel is permitted only till 10:00 PM. Late entry requires prior permission from the warden.
  • Female visitors are not allowed inside the hostel rooms.
  • Residents must inform the warden in case of night-outs or leaves.
  • Use of electrical appliances (heaters, irons, etc.) is allowed only in designated areas.

  • Gym and ground facilities are open from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
  • Proper sports/gym attire is mandatory.
  • Gym equipments must be placed at their designated place.
  • Equipment must be handled with care and returned after use.
  • Food and beverages are not allowed inside the gym or on the ground.
  • Any injury or accident must be reported immediately to the hostel authorities.
